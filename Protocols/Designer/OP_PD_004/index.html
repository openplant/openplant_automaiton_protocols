Transformation Spreading to 6 Well Plates

Description

This set of protocols transfers samples from a 96 well plate to 6-well plates containing solid media.

There are four separate protocols which carry out the transfer of 24 samples to four 6-well plates, resulting in 96 samples being transferred to sixteen 6-well plates.

Tips and best practices

Carry out a test run of the protocol using the labware and water to ensure that the positioning of labware and tips is suitable. Re-calibration of the deck or labware may be required.

Label the 6-well plates clearly to ensure the traceability of the samples from the PCR plate to the 6-well plates.

Following completion of the protocol(s) gently roll the plates to ensure that the sample is spread across the solid media.
